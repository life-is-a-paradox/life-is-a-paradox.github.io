Life is a Paradox

A Poem About Contradictions, Suffering, and the Hope Beyond

Life is a paradox. To some, it feels like paradise; to others, it burns like hell. For some, life is all they hold onto — for others, death is all they crave. Perspective shapes it, and situation defines it. To me, life is a test, and death is not the end. Be aware — there is something beyond the paradox. To some, that thought is comforting; to others, it is unsettling. Those who feel paradise in this world may lose their way — though not all do. Those who suffer through hell may find the true path — though not all will. For truth is not guaranteed by pain, nor is it denied by ease. The paradox remains… but beyond it, something greater waits.

By Monir Islam
